BlueCushion

BlueCushion is a co-working space that fuses work and meditation. Ideal for writers, bloggers, software developers, web designers, job-seekers, or anyone looking for a quiet workspace that incorporates structured meditation during the workday.

We're located in downtown Manhattan at 64 Fulton Street, which is accessible by the We're open from 9:30am till 4:30pm Monday thru Friday. We meditate twice a day, at 11:00am and 3:00pm. Each sitting session lasts fifteen minutes. We also stresses limited talking during the workday. BlueCushion is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
