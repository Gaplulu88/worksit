BlueCushion

BlueCushion is a co-working space that fuses work and meditation. Our mission is to create a quiet workspace that allows creative work and meditation to co-exist seamlessly. Our space is ideal for writers, bloggers, software developers, web designers, or anyone looking for a quiet workspace that incorporates structured meditation during the workday.

Our main meditation practice will be to simply follow our breath. We are looking increase our mindfullness and awareness, as well as strenghten our sense of inner peace. We meditate twice a day, at 11:00am and 3:00pm. Each sitting session will last 30 minutes and on occasion will incorporate walking meditation. Meditation instruction will be provided. We stress limited talking during the workday.
