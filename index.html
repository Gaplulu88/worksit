BlueCushion

BlueCushion is a co-working space that fuses work and meditation. Our space is ideal for writers, bloggers, software developers, web designers, job-seekers, or anyone looking for a quiet workspace that incorporates structured meditation during the workday. We strongly encourage silence during the workday and full participation in our meditation sessions. BlueCushion was started by experienced meditators who's ultimate mission is to create an atmosphere where people can live a truly contemplative life.

We're located in downtown Manhattan at 64 Fulton Street, which is accessible by the 2, 3, 4, 5, A, C, J, Z and R lines. We meditate twice a day, at 11:00am and 3:00pm. Each sitting session lasts fifteen minutes. We also stresses limited talking during the workday. BlueCushion is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
